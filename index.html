Research Interests

    Our human language holds rich but complicated signals that traditional methods might overlook. Leveraging the power of AI technology, I am dedicated to deciphering these subtle cues embedded within our words and expressions. My work is centered on developing innovative computational methods and data-driven linguistic variables. These tools don't just decode language—they illuminate it, providing a wealth of empirical insights for Finance, Accounting, and Communication. Such revelations offer fresh perspectives and solid foundations for theory development.

  • Research Methods: Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning, Econometrics
